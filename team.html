Team

MEET OUR BUZZCAPTURE TEAM.

At Buzzcapture, we work together with a team of passionate employees. Our developers enjoy big data, crawling technology and data visualization. Our media analysts love personal contact, advising about analyses and customer care or a research report with results that really helps our client. We share the same workplace that ensures the exchange of our knowledge, experiences and of course humor.

As the founder of Buzzcapture, he obviously has an obsession with technological development in new media. Alex specializes in reputation management for businesses and focuses on understanding the wishes of his clients. For Buzzcapture, Alex defines our strategy and makes sure everyone is in the right place and doing the best they can. What makes Alex Alex? Alex is a designer, techie, sales manager and analyst. Or at least, that’s what he thinks he is…
